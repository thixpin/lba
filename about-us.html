About Company

London Business Academy is a very first private institute in Mandalay which is founded in 2017 in business partnership with reputable and well-known accreditations both from UK and Asia.

LBA is delivering accredited qualifications, and offering courses from recognized professional bodies and also offer a range of academic qualifications in "Entrepreneurship, Business Management, Marketing, Project Management, Human Resource Management and Leadership" in terms of Awards, Certificates, Diplomas, Bachelor and Master Programs.

With the best library in town, quality teaching and variety of programs, high-class campus facility, reputable student service, decent medical care, fitness center for students, and reliability for your career, LBA stands as the finest choice for students delivering world class education.
